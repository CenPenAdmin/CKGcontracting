About Custer & Kinney

Building West Virginia's future, one project at a time

Our Story

Founded in 2003, Custer & Kinney General Contracting LLC has been serving the communities of West Virginia for over two decades. What started as a small partnership between two friends with a shared vision has grown into one of the region's most trusted construction companies.

Our founders, Mike Custer and Jim Kinney, both born and raised in West Virginia, understood the unique challenges and opportunities that come with building in the Mountain State. From the rugged terrain to the close-knit communities, they built their business on the values that matter most to West Virginians: honesty, hard work, and quality craftsmanship.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing exceptional construction and renovation services that exceed our clients' expectations. Every project, whether it's a small repair or a complete home renovation, receives the same attention to detail and commitment to quality that has made us a leader in the industry.
